Get to Know Pearce-Blackburn

We have a rich history of service in Central Kentucky



Pearce-Blackburn Roofing was founded in 1969 by Bill Pearce to meet the needs of clients looking for a quality installer who will stand behind their work. Bill has always put the client first and after 56 years we still uphold these values with honest work, intergrity and character.

The Pearce-Blackburn management team has over 165 years experience in commercial roofing. This enables us to provide a solution for any roof system needed. We staff over 30 experienced installers to provide roof services ranging from leak detection to full-roof replacement.

Pearce-Blackburn represents only the highest quality manufacturers in the market including: Carlisle, Firestone, GAF, Johns Manville, Garland, Soprema, CertainTeed and Tremco. Our goal at Pearce-Blackburn is to provide our clients with the most effective and economic solution to ensure they receive the maximum life out of their roof system.

We can say with much confidence that we have some of the best professionals in the industry today. Our field personnel has up to 35 years experience in all forms of roofing, and they are the backbone to our success. Contact us today for a quote and let us earn more than your business, let us earn a relationship for years to come!
